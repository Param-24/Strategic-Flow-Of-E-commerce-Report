Give Discounts


Offering bargains can motivate web traffic to buy products -- it works wonders for acquiring new customers.
Not only are discounts catchy, but they also make audience feel good about buying.



To get better results out of discounts, mention them in strategic places on website.
If running other types of marketing strategies, pay close attention to timing and in which location of marketing funnel it will present discounts.
With the right location, strategy, timing, and discount offer, conversion rate will increase.
